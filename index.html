Features

Everything you need to find your co-builder

Swipe-to-Match Cards

Tinder-inspired card deck lets you quickly decide who you want to connect with. Like, pass, or super-like in one tap.

Project-Centric Profiles

Profiles focus on what you build, how you work, and what you need — not your follower count or job title.

Instant Matched DMs

When both sides show interest, a conversation opens immediately. No cold emails, no awkward intros.

Incoming Interest Tab

See who liked your project or profile in a dedicated tab. No more missed connections — stay in control of your inbound.

Work Style Signals

Filter by async vs. sync workers, time zones, and collaboration preferences so you match on more than just skills.

Mobile-First Experience

Built for thumbs. Lightweight, fast, and designed for the way real builders browse — on their phones, on the go.
