Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

KindLink collects the information needed to run the app and waitlist. If you join the waitlist, we collect your email address so we can contact you about launch updates.

In the app, KindLink may collect account details, profile information, project details, photos, messages, matches, swipe activity, reports, blocked-user preferences, approximate or precise location if you allow it, and app interaction data.

We use this data to create accounts, show relevant people and projects, support messaging and matches, improve safety, prevent abuse, provide customer support, and improve the product.

We use trusted services including Firebase, Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, Formspree for waitlist submissions, and Google AdMob for sponsored advertising. AdMob may receive app, device, and ad interaction information needed to serve, measure, and improve ads.

KindLink does not sell your personal information. You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
